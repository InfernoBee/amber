How to Play ❓

⚔️ Combat

Tap the timing bar when the marker is in the glowing sweet spot for a Critical Hit! Missing still deals normal damage.

🌟 Skills

Slash Arc – Powerful strike, 6s cooldown.
Shield Guard – Reduces next hit by 50%, 10s cooldown.
Heal – Restore 20% HP, 15s cooldown.

🎒 Gear

Equip Weapon, Armor, and Ring to boost your stats. Gear comes in Common → Rare → Epic → Legendary rarities.

📜 Quests

Complete Story Chapters for permanent rewards and unlock new classes. Check Daily Quests every day for bonus XP!

👗 Wardrobe

Unlock cosmetic skins and accessories through quests and lucky drops.

💾 Save

Progress is saved automatically after every run.
